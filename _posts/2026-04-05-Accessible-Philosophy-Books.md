These are texts that take themselves intellectually seriously but also are aimed at a broader audience. These are roughly in order of how accessible these books are, but your mileage may vary!

Not Strictly Philosophy:
* [Why Fish Don’t Exist: A Story of Loss, Love, and the Hidden Order of Life by Lulu Miller](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/50887097-why-fish-don-t-exist): Philosophy of Evolution
* [Eating Animals by Jonathan Safran Foer](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/7294052-eating-animals): Philosophy of Food, Ethics
* [Being Mortal: Medicine and What Matters in the End by Atul Gawande](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/20696006-being-mortal): Philsophy of Death and Aging, Ethics
* [The Other Significant Others: Reimagining Life with Friendship at the Center Rhaina Cohen](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/127305688-the-other-significant-others): Philosophy of Love, Ethics
* [How to Do Nothing: Resisting the Attention Economy by Jenny Odell](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/42771901-how-to-do-nothing)
* [The Righteous Mind: Why Good People Are Divided by Politics and Religion by Jonathan Haidt](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/11324722-the-righteous-mind): Moral Psychology, Ethics
* [Ace: What Asexuality Reveals About Desire, Society, and the Meaning of Sex by Angela Chen](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/52128695-ace): Philosophy of Sexuality

Philosophy:
* [Assholes: A Theory by Aaron James](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/13517138-assholes): Moral Psychology, Ethics
* [What Love Is: And What It Could Be by Carrie Jenkins](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/29502349-what-love-is): Philosophy of Love, Ethics
* [The Meaning of Travel: Philosophers Abroad by Emily Thomas](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/52049847-the-meaning-of-travel): Philosophy of Travel, Ethics
* [The Right to Sex: Feminism in the Twenty-First Century by Amia Srinivasan](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/56347680-the-right-to-sex): Philosophy of Sex, Feminist Theory, Ethics, Philosophy of Law
* [Sad Love: Romance and the Search for Meaning by Carrie Jenkins](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/61608614-sad-love): Philosophy of Love, Ethics
* [The Grasshopper: Games, Life, and Utopia by Bernard Suits](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/803547.The_Grasshopper): Philosophy of Games, Ethics
* [Aftermath: Violence and the Remaking of a Self by Susan J. Brison](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/129686.Aftermath): Philosophy of Trauma, Metaphysics, Ethics
