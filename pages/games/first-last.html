Rules: First-Last is a word game where you are given two letters: one is the first letter of the word and the other is the last letter of the word. You have to give a word which satisfies these conditions in the quickest time possible!

Instructions: Submit your word by pressing the Enter key. Use the Start/Stop Game button to get a new letter pair.

Tip: If you want to make your game harder, try raising the minimum word length, or restrict your usage of proper nouns.
