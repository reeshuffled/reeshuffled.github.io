Inspired by [Tracy Durnell's page](https://tracydurnell.com/questions/) of the same name.

A lot of my writing is an effort to understand the world around me. These questions are not always at the forefront of my mind while writing, but I think they do help trace an undercurrent to a lot of my work on this site.

I have the more central questions bolded, with links to the anthology or flagship posts that explore each one.

**How can we connect to people (at scale)?**

Building real community — whether hosting gatherings, making and keeping friends, or sharing your life "in public" — is something I return to constantly. See: [Personal Community Building](/anthologies/personal-community-building), [Hosting for Emerging Adults](/anthologies/hosting-for-emerging-adults)

**Can we reimagine romance and relationships beyond the default script?**

From romantic abolitionism to relationship anarchy, I'm interested in whether the social institution of romance is actually serving us, and what we might replace or supplement it with. See: [Abolishing Romance](/anthologies/abolishing-romance), [Reimagining Relationships](/anthologies/reimagining-relationships), [Normative Relationships](/anthologies/normative-relationships)

**What does a-spec identity reveal about desire, attraction, and relationships?**

My A-Spec identity has been a lens for rethinking attraction and desire, and for imagining what relationships can look like when they aren't organized around romance and sex. See: [Perspectives on A-Spec Identity](/anthologies/perspectives-on-a-spec-identity)

**How can we love and relate to one another better?**

The practical and philosophical work of being a better friend, partner, and person in relationship — from building relationship skills to what mutual support in friendship actually looks like. See: [How To Build Relationship Skills]({% post_url 2025-11-12-How-To-Build-Relationship-Skills %}), [Mutual Support in Friendship]({% post_url 2025-07-05-Mutual-Support-in-Friendship %})

**How can we get more people to eat plant-based?**

I've been Vegan at Home for over two years and I keep coming back to questions of how to make reducetarianism more accessible and appealing to more people. See: [Reducetarian]({% post_url 2024-10-29-Reducetarian %}), [The Effects of Different Reducetarian Splits]({% post_url 2025-01-03-The-Effects-of-Different-Reducetarian-Splits %})

**How can we make philosophy more accessible?**

Philosophy gives people tools to think more clearly and live more deliberately, but it's kept behind too many barriers. I want more people to have access to it. See: [Democratizing Philosophy]({% post_url 2024-11-04-Democratizing-Philosophy %}), [Expanding the Market for Philosophy]({% post_url 2026-04-28-Expanding-The-Market-for-Philosophy %}), [Accessible Philosophy Books]({% post_url 2026-04-05-Accessible-Philosophy-Books %})

**When and how is it acceptable to use AI?**

I'm skeptical of a lot of AI hype but interested in building principled frameworks for when and how it's ethical and useful to use it. See: [AI Usage Acceptability Spectrum]({% post_url 2025-11-19-AI-Usage-Acceptability-Spectrum %}), [Thoughts and Questions Around AI Adoption]({% post_url 2026-02-13-Thoughts-and-Questions-Around-AI-Adoption %})

**How can we own our digital lives and build things that last?**

IndieWeb principles, personal data ownership, and building software and websites that are durable and genuinely yours. See: [Owning My Data]({% post_url 2024-07-21-Owning-My-Data %}), [Don't Break Links](/posts/dont-break-links), [Build a website that lasts]({% post_url 2026-05-06-Build-a-website-that-lasts %})

**How can we live more slowly and intentionally?**

Designing daily life with intention rather than defaulting to busyness and autopilot — and building systems that free up mental energy for what actually matters. See: [Life System Development](/anthologies/life-system-development), [Living Slower and More Intentionally]({% post_url 2023-02-26-Living-Slower-and-More-Intentionally %})

**How can we teach children ethical principles in order to accelerate their moral development?**

Early ethics education as a foundation for being a thoughtful and empathetic member of society. See: [Teaching Children Ethics]({% post_url 2025-02-23-Teaching-Children-Ethics %})

**What is play — and why do humor, flirting, and riffing matter?** *(emerging)*

A nascent thread on the philosophy of playful interaction — teasing, comedic exchange, and what flirting actually is. See: [What is play?]({% post_url 2025-08-24-What-is-play %}), [Teasing, Play, and Flirtation]({% post_url 2025-10-08-Teasing-Play-and-Flirtation %}), [Riffing is Human Nature]({% post_url 2025-11-26-Riffing-is-Human-Nature %})
